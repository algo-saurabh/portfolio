UnType is an AI-based data automation tool, which helps in content creation with zero human intervention. It can understand and rewrite any pdf, image, or even handwritten files and replicate it, where the data is not only editable but also downloadable in various formats and use elsewhere and everywhere.

Objective

Designing untype.ai from scratch which is AI based tool that will be used to minimize the efforts of independent educators / data entry operators in creating, sharing and exporting the content.

Approach

My Contribution

Since I was the only designer working this project, I was responsible to design the entire product from scratch. I started with preliminary research, gathered the requirements, created Information Architecture, User flows, wireframes and then finally the Hi-Fidelity screens.
