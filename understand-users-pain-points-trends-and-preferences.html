Cake Time - application to order perfect cake

Duration:

3 weeks

Team:

Tanya, Harsha, Snehal

Role:

UX Designer, Front-End Developer

The emergence of online food delivery services such as Zomato and Swiggy has fundamentally changed the way consumers are purchasing food. Despite the eCommerce industry experiencing double digital growth year over year, there are still many products that are not traditionally viewed as items that are sold online. Cakes and bakery goods are one of the non-traditional items.

Objective

The goal of the project is to create an e-commerce store for cakes, the concept is to sell cakes online wherein very niche preferences of the user is also taken into consideration to provide with personalized user experience. The online store must allow the consumer to experience the same or similar sensations that they would receive in store with attention to care while creating a personalized experience.

Approach

My Contribution

This project was done as a part of the coursework, collaborating with two of my classmates. My role in this project was to take lead in writing the code to develop the designed app in android studio since I had prior experience in coding. I was also actively involved in the research and design phases of the project.
