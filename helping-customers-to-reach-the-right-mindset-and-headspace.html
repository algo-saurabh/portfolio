Interactive learning platform that aims to provide a holistic environment to the teacher as well as the students by utilizing features powered by AI and designing them in a way that it can easily accessed by students as well as teachers.

Objective

To design an interactive learning platform that aims to provide a holistic environment to the teacher as well as the students by utilizing features like live polling, discussion mode, real-time analysis of student's performance, sound-enabled hand raising, permission required to disable video, a rotating camera keeping an eye on every student, computerized division of groups for group activities, and a record book with class recordings, students participation data. Parents will also be more aware of their ward's performance from a broader perceptive, including active participation, talk time, valuable inputs, leadership qualities, and weaknesses.
